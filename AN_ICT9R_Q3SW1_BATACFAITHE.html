About the Show:

Emily in Paris follows Emily Cooper, an American college graduate in her late twenties with a Master's degree in communication who is from Chicago; she moves to Paris for an unexpected job opportunity. She is tasked with bringing an American point of view and social media presence to a venerable French marketing firm. Cultures clash as she adjusts to the challenges of life in Paris while juggling her career, new friendships, and active love life.

Emily

Born Alfred in Hampshire, England, he is a sibling to three brothers. He enjoyed finances and football growing up. He learned to balance his work life and personal one. After Brexit, he was sent by a UK bank to assist a French branch in financial services concerned the post-Brexit transition. He was picked to live in Paris as he's the only person in his office without a wife or family to uproot during the transition process. A condition of his move is his requirement to attend French classes, which he loathes entirely, along with the rest of the Paris culture.

Camille

Camille is sweet, sophisticated and the daughter of successful French champagne company owners. She takes Emily under her wing to show her that Paris isn't as big as it seems, quickly becoming one of her closest confidantes. Camille was the girlfriend of Gabriel before they broke up.

Gabriel

Gabriel was born into a middle-class family in Brionne, Normandy. His family owned a farm so he had a simplistic lifestyle. His entire family raised tuition money so he could attend cooking school in Paris. Upon graduating, Gabriel became the chef at Les Deux Compères.

Mindy

Mindy Chen is a resident of Paris who originally lived in Shanghai. Quick-witted nanny and aspiring singer Mindy becomes fast friends with Emily after they cross paths in a park. Mindy’s move to Paris redefined her relationship with her family and the separation takes an emotional toll. Still, she's a dreamer — and searching for the courage to step into the spotlight.

Nicholas

Nicolas de Leon is the crown prince and dutiful executive of his family’s high powered conglomerate, JVMA. Nicolas seems to lead a charmed life. But while the family name is an endless source of wealth, status, and access, it’s accompanied by a crushing pressure to prove himself as more than just a nepotism hire.

Benoit

Benoît is a guitar player who busks with Étienne and Mindy. He and Étienne invite Mindy to join their band after hearing her sing at a French cabaret club.
